What is LocAIly?

Making AI accessible and secure for businesses through local deployment

Founded by Harkirat Rakhraj, a recent graduate of Ivey Business School with a Masters of Science in Digital Management, LocAIly emerged from a simple observation: businesses want AI solutions but hesitate due to data privacy concerns.

We implement AI solutions that run entirely on your infrastructure. No data leaves your premises, no cloud dependencies, and no unexpected costs.
